Skydiving Facts

The youngest person to ever skydive was Joshua Pine, who jumped from 10,000 feet strapped to his dad at only four years old!

Alfred Blaschke skydived at the age of 103 years old, which is the record for the oldest person to every skydive!

In 2006, Jay Stokes completed 640 skydive jumps in one day, setting a world record!

In Udon Thani, Thailand the largest skydiving formation was done with 400 people and was done with assistance from the Royal Thai Air Force

The highest any person has jumped from is 128,100 feet! This was done by Felix Baumgartner in 2012 with assistance from Red Bull
